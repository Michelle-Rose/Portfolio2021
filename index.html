Whilst working at Concentrix call centre supporting the everyday users with technical issues on their personal devices my interest in information technology was rekindled. From supporting the call centres customers, I was exposed to end-users their user experiences and security concerns. From listening to the concerns and helping to solve their inquiries an interest was sparked in user experience design and information security. With these interests in mind, I made the decision to return to study at Whitireia. I now want to continuously learn more about UX UI Design to be able to design products with the user experience in mind. I also have a passion for security and want to learn more about ethical hacking/ pen-testing and be up to date on information technology trends and security standards. Ideally would like to work as a front end developer or UX/UI Designer.

My experience in customer support makes me different from others. While working in the call centre I learnt a lot of life skills from being able to work in a team, be able to communicate, to being organised and to managing expectations of customers and coworkers. I will be able to take these skills I have learnt into any new role and I will happily take the opportunity to be able to develop these skills further
Alongside studying I worked part-time for New Zealand Uniform as a Website Coordinator. Whilst working at NZU I was able to apply these skills when working within the team of upper management, customer service and outsource tech company Native software. I was given the opportunity to redesign their website in order to help reduce customer service chats. NZU was experiencing high chat requests of customers looking for a specific school or club uniform. In order to reduce the chat volumes, I put forward redesign ideas of homepage layout and navigation. These ideas were generated after talking to upper management, customer service and sales/retail staff about how they use the site and what they wanted. This opportunity gave me my first paid-for experience of applying design skills I learnt at polytech and my design were implemented in December 2019 and they successfully reduced the chat requests by 98%.
